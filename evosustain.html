1. Introduction to EvoSustain Ltd.

EvoSustain Ltd. is an innovative, eco-friendly software platform designed to optimise waste collection (garbage, debris, objects) through smart route planning technology. The application enables efficient management of on-demand waste collection for both public and private sector companies, helping to reduce environmental impact, lower CO2 emissions, and improve operational efficiency.

Vision of EvoSustain Ltd.

To become the leading platform in intelligent waste management, assisting waste collection companies in operating more efficiently and sustainably, contributing to the development of smart and green cities.

Mission of EvoSustain Ltd.

To provide innovative software solutions that optimise waste collection operations, enhance sustainability, and offer detailed insights into environmental impact for businesses of all sizes.

2. Key Features of the Application

Main Features:

  • Smart Route Optimisation: Advanced algorithms to reduce travel distances and collection times, minimising fuel consumption and CO2 emissions.
  • Collection Request Management: Customers can activate on-demand collections via smart stickers and the mobile app, or when closing their business.
  • Route Planning System: Drivers receive optimised routes through the app, improving operational efficiency and cutting costs.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Managers can monitor real-time driver routes and assign tasks based on demand, ensuring high availability and flexibility.
  • Detailed Reports: The app generates efficiency reports and sustainability data, showing environmental impact, waste types, areas of high impact, CO2 emissions, etc.
  • Multi-Platform Interface: The application is available for mobile devices for drivers and a web version for company managers.
  • Biodegradable Stickers with QR Codes: Each customer receives a biodegradable sticker with a QR code that allows them to activate the waste collection request precisely. The customer simply scans the QR code to activate the service, and the code is deactivated automatically when the driver collects the waste, debris, or objects. This system ensures precise location tracking and enhances operational efficiency by confirming collection at the correct location.

3. Benefits of EvoSustain Ltd.

For Waste Collection Companies:

  • Cost Reduction: Route optimisation reduces travel time and fuel consumption, increasing profitability.
  • Improved Efficiency: Automatic route planning and task assignment reduce human error and improve punctuality.
  • Enhanced Visibility: Access to detailed reports on driver performance, waste types, and emissions.
  • Improved Sustainability: Reduction in CO2 emissions and better control over environmental operations.

For Clients:

  • Simple Activation: Clients can activate the collection via a smart sticker and QR code, ensuring the service is activated only when needed.
  • On-Demand Collection: The option for clients to have waste collected either when required or when closing their business.
  • Direct Feedback: Clients can provide feedback on the service received, helping improve quality.

For Cities:

  • Environmental Impact Reduction: By optimising routes, CO2 emissions are reduced, contributing to a cleaner, more sustainable city.
  • Better Waste Management: With real-time monitoring, cities can manage waste collection more efficiently.

4. Business Models

  • SaaS Subscription Model: Basic, Standard, and Corporate Plans with monthly pricing based on the volume of collection requests.
  • Pay-Per-Use: Fee per collection request activated through the platform.
  • Perpetual Licence: One-time payment for the software license.
  • Strategic Partnerships & Franchises: Expansion opportunities through partnerships with local governments or franchises operating in different regions.
