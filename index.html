Evoford Holdings

Evoford centralizes the operations of EvoSustain and Marketford. We help optimize business operations through advanced technology, enabling companies to achieve greater efficiency and sustainable growth. Through specialized consulting and advice, we guide businesses in adapting to a greener, more responsible future aligned with sustainability principles and environmental care.

Evosustain

EvoSustain develops innovative software to enhance sustainability and efficiency for businesses. Our mission is to implement advanced technological solutions that optimize operational processes, reduce costs, and, most importantly, decrease CO₂ emissions. In our first year of operation in London, we are committed to reducing pollution and improving efficiency by 40%, contributing to a greener, cleaner, and more sustainable city. With a focus on innovation and environmental commitment, we work to create a healthier and more sustainable future for urban communities, contributing to a greener world for future generations.

Marketford

Marketford is the owner of the private brand TO BE LUXURY, offering products through our growing online platform, ensuring an exceptional shopping experience across Europe. Additionally, Marketford will supply materials for environmental campaigns, tools, and resources aimed at supporting the London community in its efforts to promote sustainability and environmental care.

What is Evosustain and how does it benefit society?

Evosustain is our flagship initiative focused on developing eco-friendly software solutions for waste management and sustainability. By optimizing waste collection routes and enhancing operational efficiency, we help reduce environmental impact and improve city-wide waste management practices, benefiting both the environment and communities.
