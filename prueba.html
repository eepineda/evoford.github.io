EvoSustain Pilot & POC Plan

Discover the benefits of EvoSustain’s innovative waste management solutions with our tailored pilot and proof of concept for your business.

Why Pilot with EvoSustain?

Our pilot program lets you experience firsthand how EvoSustain's technology can revolutionize your waste collection operations, improving efficiency, sustainability, and cost-effectiveness.

  • Test real-time route optimization and task assignment
  • Gain detailed visibility of driver performance and collection status
  • Receive actionable sustainability reports and CO₂ impact data
  • Engage your team with easy-to-use mobile apps for stores and drivers
  • Access support and training from EvoSustain experts throughout the pilot

Pilot Program Details

The pilot is designed for quick deployment with measurable results:

  • Duration: 3 months with full access to all modules
  • Participants: Up to 50 stores, 15 drivers, and admin users
  • Comprehensive training and onboarding support
  • Customizable KPIs and reports tailored to your goals
  • Feedback loops to adapt and enhance the solution during the pilot

Key Modules Included

  • Stores App: Quick collection requests, QR code scanning, tracking, and history views.
  • Business Admin Portal: Request approvals, schedule planning, driver assignments, and analytics.
  • Drivers App: Daily route overview, task details, status updates, and navigation support.
